WatchHer – Account and Data Deletion Request

This page explains how users of WatcHer can request the deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request account deletion

To request the deletion of your WatcHer account and associated data, please follow these steps:

- Send an email to watchercheilspain@gmail.com. Use the same email address that you used to create your WatchHer account

- Include the subject line: "Account Deletion Request – WatcHer"

Once your request is received, we may contact you to verify account ownership in order to prevent unauthorized deletion.

What data will be deleted

After verification, we will permanently delete the following data associated with your account:

- Your WatchHer user account (Firebase Authentication)

- Account configuration and onboarding preferences

- Tracking sessions created within the app

- Audio recordings collected during tracking or alarm sessions

- Location data associated with tracking or alarm sessions

Data retention

Account deletion requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe.

Once deletion is completed, the data listed above is permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

No personal data is retained after account deletion, except where required by law.
